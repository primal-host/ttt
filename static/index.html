Rules
  • levels of play
  • Nine tic-tac-toe boards arranged in a 3×3 grid
  • You are green, the computer is red
  • Your cell position determines which board your opponent plays next
  • If sent to a full board, your opponent plays anywhere
  • Won boards stay playable until full
  • Win three boards in a row to win
  • Draw if no three-in-a-row is possible
